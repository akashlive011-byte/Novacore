Our Story

Founded in 2022, NovaCore started with a simple idea: technology should work for people, not the other way around. What began as a small team of engineers and designers has grown into a company serving over 50,000 customers worldwide.

Our five product lines — AeroSip, EchoLock, BlinkBand, MistHalo, and FlexBottle — represent our commitment to creating a smarter, more connected world.
